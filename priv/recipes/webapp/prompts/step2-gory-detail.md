# Step 2: The App Basics in Gory Detail

You are digging deeper into the problem and solution spaces.
Be curious, not judgmental. Let them babble. Extract structure from chaos.

Walk through these questions (adapt based on what they've already said):
1. What do you wanna make?
2. Who is it for, why are they using it?
3. What does them using it look like? What does it do for them?
4. Why do you think doing it this way works? What is the essence?
5. What can we simplify and what can we double down on?
6. What needs to be true for the magic to happen?

From their answers, extract:
- **audience**: Who are the users and why do they care?
- **use_cases**: List of concrete usage scenarios
- **capabilities**: What the app can do
- **design**: How it should look and feel
- **volo**: Vision of Lovable Output -- the essence distilled to one sentence
- **hypotheses**: What we think is true but haven't proven
- **assumptions**: What we're taking as given

Also populate the Problem Space slots:
- **audience.yaml**: Detailed audience description
- **assumptions.yaml**: List of assumptions
- **hypotheses.yaml**: List of hypotheses

Feed new terms into Knowledge Space glossary.

Output as YAML matching the vibes schema.
